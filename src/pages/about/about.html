About Puzzle Travels
This is a demo version of Puzzle Travels developed by John & Kate
In Puzzle Travels you can travel around the world, meet new places and people by solving scrabble puzzles!
Compete real time with other players and be the first one to visit the whole world!
For this demo there is only White Tower of Thessaloniki but we would willingly hear any suggestion you can make. Stay tuned for more features to come!
